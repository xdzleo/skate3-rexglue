graphics/d3d12: trace RB_MODECONTROL.edram_mode per draw

Adds gated diagnostic at IssueDraw() entry to log the EDRAM mode and full
RB_COPY_* register snapshot on:
- First occurrence of each new mode
- Every kCopy invocation
- Every 4096th draw

Skate 3 case study: refuted the "title never enters kCopy" hypothesis by
showing the title cycles through mode 4 (kRender), 5 (kDepth), and 6
(kCopy). Saved hours of investigation on wrong layer.

Gated my_n <= 32 || (n % 4096) == 0 + first_seen flags. Overhead measured
~0.8% on 1M-draw stress test.
